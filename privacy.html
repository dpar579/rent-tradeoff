By creating an account or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with it, please do not use the Service.

2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account information: email address, username, and password (stored in encrypted/hashed form and never visible to us in plain text).
  • Preference data: the comparison criteria you configure and the weighting you assign to each one.
  • Property data: nicknames, scores, and notes you enter for rental properties you compare.
  • Billing information: if you unlock the paid tier (unlimited listings from your 4th saved property onward), payment is processed by a third-party payment processor. RentBest does not store your full card number or payment credentials — only limited transaction metadata (e.g. subscription status, plan, payment date) is retained.
  • Technical and usage data: IP address, browser type, device information, log data, and cookie identifiers, collected automatically when you use the Service.

3. How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and authenticate your account, and keep you signed in across sessions;

  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Service;
  • Communicate with you about your account, changes to the Service, or in response to a support request;
  • Comply with our legal obligations, and detect or prevent fraud and abuse; and
